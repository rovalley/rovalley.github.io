Showdown Montana is a mountain ski area that is located near Neihart. The drive to Showdown has amazing scenery and stops. It is fairly cheap per person and has great gear you can rent if you do not own any yourself. The slopes are really fun to hit, and the terrain park has everything you would want. Overall all it is a good experience, and if you've never been I would recommend checking it out.
