Activities Home Page

Football is a fun contact sport whether you like to play offense or defense. It is also entertaining to watch and have a favorite team to root for. Basketball is cool to see how individual players change the way the game is played by their own skill. Also the Finals are always entertaining becuasue there is more than one game and anything can happen. Snowboarding has its ups and downs but once you master riding down the slopes, you can hit the ramps and get airborn. It is fun to go with your friends and ride all day long. Riding a wave is exhilarating when its a good one and you can ride it all the way back to shore. Surfing is fun but is very exhausting be prepared when trying to hit the waves. The four activities are entertaining to watch and do. Each take skill and time to master but is worth it.
