Web Hosts Review

justhost.com

Justhost cost $3.95 a month. Domain services include unlimited Add-On Domains, unlimited Parked Domains, and unlimited Subdomains. Web hosting features include FREE Email Address, FREE Marketing Tools, FREE Website Builder.

fatcow.com

Fatcow cost $4.08 a month. Domain services include Free transferring of domains and free migration of sites from other hosts to FatCow, Manage unlimited domain names, Automatic renewal of domains. Web hosting include VPS hosting, Dedicated hosting, and WordPress hosting.

hostgator.com

Hostgator cost $2.75 a month, domain services include register domains, tranfer domains, and manage domains. Webhosting includes cloud hosting, Application hosting, and Windows hosting.

Best Choice

I am choosing gatorhost because it has many plans ranging from cheap to expense depending on what you are looking for.
